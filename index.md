# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 10, 2026

## Bloom Path Privacy Policy

Bloom Path ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how Bloom Path collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

By using Bloom Path, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. About Bloom Path

Bloom Path is an educational application designed to support children through interactive learning activities, visual schedules, educational videos, and progress tracking. Parent and caregiver accounts can manage child profiles and monitor learning progress.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, we may collect:

### Account Information

* Parent or guardian email address
* Account authentication information

### Child Profile Information

* Child's first name or nickname
* Avatar selection
* Date of birth (if provided)
* Learning preferences
* Difficulty settings
* Accessibility preferences (such as Low Stimulation Mode)

### Learning Progress

* Completed activities
* Game scores
* Accuracy statistics
* Time spent learning
* Daily schedule completion
* Achievement badges
* Video progress

### Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription, payments are securely processed through Google Play or the Apple App Store. Bloom Path does not store your payment card information.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

* Provide educational content
* Save learning progress
* Personalize the learning experience
* Maintain child profiles
* Improve application performance
* Provide customer support
* Manage subscriptions
* Maintain application security

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

Bloom Path is intended to be used under the supervision of a parent, guardian, therapist, or caregiver.

We encourage parents and guardians to supervise children's use of the application.

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information only when necessary with trusted service providers that help operate the application, including:
