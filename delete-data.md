# Bloom Path - Delete Your Data

Users may delete some information without deleting their Bloom Path account.

Within the app, go to:

**Profile → Settings**

Available options may include:

- Delete Child Profile
- Reset Learning Progress
- Clear Rewards
- Clear Video History
- Reset Daily Schedule

If you cannot access the app, contact:


bloom.kidzapp@gmail.com

Requests are generally completed within 30 days.
